OOIS_PCOM7E

OOIS_PCOM7E: Object-oriented information systems

The objective of this module is to explore the fundamental theories and practices of computing, and also explore the regulatory and ethical developments of this field, this aimed to to minimise computer technology related threats or possible data breaches.

Unit 1: Introduction to Information Systems

Learning outcomes
  • Understand the core elements of an information system.
  • Appreciate the common issues that can arise when deploying a system.
  • Gain an understanding of the SDLC, its phases and their importance.
