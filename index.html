// About

Aim high. These two words, although clichéd, exactly represent what my life has looked like recently. During the last decade, my experiences, goals, and professional dreams have changed radically. To me, aim high has meant going from working alone on my computer at a dimly lighted Starbucks in Mexico City to developing several flight simulators, having an office with a few employees, directing a company respected by many loyal clients, and leading a team of fifty people to transform a decommissioned Boeing 737 into a library for a marginalized neighborhood.
