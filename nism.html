Network and Information Security Management

NISM_PCOM7E: Network and Information Security Management

The objective of this module is to explore the fundamental theories and practices of computing, and also explore the regulatory and ethical developments of this field, this aimed to to minimise computer technology related threats or possible data breaches.

Unit 1: History & Definitions

Learning outcomes
  • Explain the basic principles of Information Security Management.
  • Describe the 4 tenets/ principles of Information Security Management.
  • Describe what constitutes a threat and vulnerability.
  • List several common roles within the Information Security profession.
